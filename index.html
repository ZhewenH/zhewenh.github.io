Zhewen Huang

Hi everyone, thanks for stopping by! I am a third-year CS PhD student at CUNY Graduate Center, where I am fortunate to be advised by Dr. Chia-Ling Tsai. I previously obtained my Master’s degree at Miami University under the guidance of Dr. John Femiani.

My research focused on image and video processing, generative modeling, multimodal large language models, autonomous agents, and AI for healthcare. I aim to build reliable vision-language systems that can understand, generate, and reason across complex multimodal data. I love to use what I learned to make people’s life better and easier.

Research Interests

  • Image and video processing and generation
  • Multimodal large language models and agents
  • AI for healthcare and medical imaging
